Chapter 02 · Science

We don't hide behind proprietary blends.

Every ingredient, every dose. This is the one place mechanism leads — for the customer who wants to check.

What a synbiotic is

A synbiotic is a prebiotic and a probiotic together in one serving. The prebiotic is fiber that feeds good gut bacteria. The probiotic is the bacteria itself. In most products you get one or the other, and you have to stack them. PHEEEW is both — in every pouch.

Why it matters: the probiotic alone doesn't do much if it doesn't have food to survive on. The prebiotic alone feeds whatever's already in your gut — good or bad. Together, they work like a system.

Bacillus coagulans SNZ 1969 · the strain

Most probiotics on the shelf are dead by the time you buy them — Lactobacillus and Bifidobacterium don't survive heat, shelf storage, or stomach acid. That's the dirty secret of the category.

SNZ 1969 is a spore-forming probiotic. The spore coat protects the bacteria through processing, shelf storage, and stomach acid. It reaches the gut alive. That's the whole reason we picked it.

  • Survives shelf storage — no refrigeration required
  • Survives heat — can be processed into a shelf-stable pouch without killing the bacteria
  • Survives stomach acid — spore coat intact until the intestine
  • FDA GRAS (Generally Recognized As Safe) status confirmed
  • Used in dietary supplements for 20+ years · 4+ published RCTs on the strain

Dose: 1 billion CFU per pouch.

Prebiotics · XOS + Fibersol-2

XOS (xylo-oligosaccharides) is a prebiotic fiber that selectively feeds good gut bacteria. We use a low dose on purpose — enough to feed the probiotic, not so much that it causes bloating in sensitive stomachs.

Fibersol-2 is a resistant maltodextrin. Well-tolerated, well-studied, works alongside XOS. Together they give the synbiotic base its structure.

Gut + Energy specifics pending final formulation

What we don't claim

The honest list. We're a conventional food, not a drug, and we write like it.

  • PHEEEW does not cure, treat, or prevent any disease.
  • We don't claim the product is FDA-approved — food isn't. The strain is FDA GRAS.
  • We don't say "boosts energy 300%" or "gives you more energy than coffee." The lift is real, not theatrical.
  • We don't say "clinically proven formula" — the strain is clinically studied; our finished product hasn't been run in its own RCT.
  • We don't promise weight loss, fat burn, or body-composition outcomes from green tea.
  • We don't claim to cure bloating, eliminate gas, or fix IBS. If your gut needs a doctor, see one.
