Behind the scenes

How it actually gets made.

Three rooms, three continents. LA is the brand. Naju is the factory. Seoul is the design studio. We photograph all of them because the process is the product, and because we think you should see where this stuff comes from.

The booth

10x10 canopy, a riser shelf, sample cups, a Square reader, and whichever of us is closest that weekend. Come say hi.

The factory

좋은영농조합법인 in Naju, Korea. HACCP certified, FDA registered. The line that fills every pouch. Jason visits quarterly.

The design studio

Dawon's workspace in Seoul. Where every visual decision starts. Paper, ink, a laptop, a lot of coffee.
