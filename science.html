The base

What a synbiotic is, in plain English.

Every PHEEEW pouch is built on a synbiotic — the category name for a product that combines three things: a live probiotic, a prebiotic fiber that feeds it, and the postbiotic activity that results when the first two do their job together. Probiotic is the live bacteria. Prebiotic is the food it eats. Postbiotic is what comes out the other side — short-chain fatty acids, metabolites, the actual effects on the gut. A synbiotic isn't a marketing word. It's just the acknowledgment that a probiotic without its food doesn't do much.

The strain

SNZ 1969. Why this one.

Bacillus coagulans SNZ 1969 — spore-forming probiotic

Most probiotics on shelves are dead by the time you drink them. That's the category's open secret. Traditional strains like Lactobacillus and Bifidobacterium need refrigeration, can't survive heat processing, and most of the live cells die on the way to the gut anyway — stomach acid kills them before they get where they're going.

Bacillus coagulans SNZ 1969 solves all three. It's spore-forming — the bacteria builds a protective shell around itself. That shell does three things:

  • Survives shelf storage. No fridge required. The pouch on your counter still works.
  • Survives heat. The manufacturing process doesn't kill it.
  • Survives stomach acid. Reaches the intestine alive, where it activates and goes to work.

SNZ 1969 has FDA GRAS status (Generally Recognized As Safe, GRN 597) and has been used in dietary supplements for twenty-plus years. There are four published randomized controlled trials on the strain, the most referenced being an IBS study (n=92, 500M CFU twice daily over 60 days) showing significant improvement in both constipation and diarrhea-dominant IBS. We don't claim our formula was clinically tested — the strain was. We ship 1 billion CFU per pouch, guaranteed at end of shelf life, not at the factory door.

The prebiotics

XOS and Fibersol-2. Why both.

XOS — xylo-oligosaccharides

A prebiotic fiber that's specifically food for the good bacteria in your gut — including the SNZ 1969 we just delivered. Dosed low enough that it doesn't cause the bloating some prebiotics are known for. If the probiotic is the tenant, XOS is the groceries.

Fibersol-2 — resistant maltodextrin

A soluble fiber with twenty-plus years of safety data and one of the best-tolerated fiber profiles in the category. Works alongside XOS — XOS is targeted food for gut flora, Fibersol-2 is general soluble fiber with its own digestive and metabolic benefits. Using both means we don't have to pick between feeding the probiotic and delivering fiber more broadly.

Gut + Energy specifics

The lift, on top of the base.

Green tea extract

The caffeine source. Natural caffeine at roughly 50–75mg per pouch — meaningfully less than a cup of coffee, paired with L-theanine that green tea brings along for the ride. The L-theanine is the reason this reads as a clean lift instead of a jittery one. We picked green tea over synthetic caffeine on purpose: it's the only way to get the full profile without additives.

Vitamin B6 and B12

Both are FDA-recognized as supporting normal energy metabolism — the body's process of turning food into usable energy. B12 also supports nervous system function. We use them at Good Source dosing per serving. This is nutrient-function, not a stimulant claim.

The pairing logic: a caffeine product on an empty stomach is a crash waiting to happen. A caffeine product on a synbiotic base, with real fruit and B-vitamins, is a pouch you can drink at 3pm without regretting it at 6pm. That's the whole design.

What we don't claim

An honest list.

The category has a habit of overpromising. We'd rather underpromise and have you tell a friend. Here's what PHEEEW is explicitly not going to do for you:

  • We don't cure, treat, or prevent any medical condition. If you have a diagnosis, talk to a doctor before changing anything.
  • We don't replace medical care. A pouch is a pouch. It's not a prescription, a therapist, or a second opinion.
  • We don't promise weight loss. No metabolism hacks, no fat-burning claims, no before-and-afters.
  • We don't promise an energy spike. The caffeine is real, the lift is measured, the crash is what we're trying to avoid.
  • We don't fix your sleep. If your sleep is broken, caffeine at 3pm probably isn't helping — no pouch fixes that.
  • We don't claim to be the first or the best. Synbiotic drinks exist. We think ours is good. We think you'll tell.
  • We don't say "clinically proven" about our formula. Our strain has four RCTs behind it. Our specific pouch does not.
