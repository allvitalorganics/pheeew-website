What's inside

Built around how a day actually feels.

Three things we thought about before anything else. The format, the base, the function. In that order.

The format

100mL. Shelf-stable. Fits a belt bag, a tote, a glovebox. Open-and-drink is one motion. No fridge, no blender, no powder. Five seconds and you're done.

The base

Real fruit puree — thicker than juice, drinkable, fruit-forward. Pre + probiotic synbiotic underneath. Tastes like a smoothie. Works like one too.

The function

Energy, today. A clean lift from green tea plus B-vitamins on a gut-first foundation. The caffeine is the assist. The gut is the point.
