Subscription Terms & Auto-Renewal Disclosure

Effective: April 20, 2026 · Last updated: April 20, 2026

If you choose a PHEEEW subscription, this page sets out exactly what you're agreeing to. It is written to meet the disclosure and cancellation requirements of the federal Restore Online Shoppers' Confidence Act (ROSCA, 15 U.S.C. §§ 8401–8405), the FTC's Negative Option Rule (16 C.F.R. Part 425), and the California Automatic Renewal Law (Cal. Bus. & Prof. Code §§ 17600–17606), as amended by AB-390 (2018) and AB-2863 (2022).

Clear and conspicuous disclosure

  • Automatic renewal: Your subscription will automatically charge your payment method on each scheduled billing date until you cancel.
  • Amount charged: The per-shipment price shown at checkout, plus applicable tax. This price is locked at signup unless we give you at least 7 days' advance email notice of a change.
  • Frequency: Your chosen cadence — 30, 45, or 60 days.
  • Cancel any time, one click: Log in to your account and click "Cancel subscription," or email hello@pheeew.com. No retention flow, no fees, no phone call required.
  • No cancellation fees.

2. Your affirmative consent

When you enroll in a subscription, you affirm that you have read and agree to these Subscription Terms and authorize PHEEEW (and our subscription processor, Recharge, and our payment processor, Stripe / Shopify Payments) to charge your payment method on file for each scheduled shipment until you cancel. The checkout screen presents the subscription terms adjacent to the enrollment control so you can review them before enrolling.

3. Billing and renewal

  • First charge: on the day you place the order.
  • Subsequent charges: on each renewal date according to your chosen cadence (30, 45, or 60 days from your previous shipment).
  • Amount: the product price (per shipment) plus any applicable sales tax and shipping, less any active promotional discount. Charges appear on your statement as "PHEEEW" or "PHEEEW.COM."
  • Duration: your subscription continues until you cancel. There is no minimum commitment.

4. How to cancel

You can cancel your subscription at any time. In accordance with Cal. Bus. & Prof. Code § 17602(c) and AB-390, cancellation is available in at least the same medium and with the same ease as enrollment.

  • Online, one click: Log in to your account at pheeew.com, go to "Manage subscription," and click "Cancel." No questions, no additional steps.
  • By email: Email hello@pheeew.com with the subject line "Cancel subscription" and the email address on your account. We will process the cancellation within one (1) business day and send a confirmation email.

Cancellations received at least 24 hours before your next scheduled charge will prevent that charge. Cancellations received within 24 hours of a scheduled charge may apply starting with the following cycle.

5. Skip, pause, change cadence

You can skip an individual shipment, pause your subscription for up to 90 days, change your cadence, change your quantity, or update your address or payment method any time in your account dashboard.

6. Changes to price or material terms

We will notify you by email at least 7 days before any change to your subscription price, renewal cadence, or other material term. The notice will describe the change, the date it takes effect, and how to cancel before it applies. If the change is one you did not consent to under your original enrollment, it will not apply to you until you accept it (in accordance with Cal. Bus. & Prof. Code § 17602(b)).

7. Payment failures

If a scheduled charge fails, our subscription processor will retry the payment method up to three times over seven days. If all retries fail, we will pause your subscription and notify you by email. You can update your payment method in your account dashboard to resume.

8. Shipping

Subscription shipments follow the Shipping Policy. Standard shipping is free on subscription orders of 30 or more pouches per shipment.

9. Refunds

Refunds on subscription orders follow our Refund & Returns Policy. The first-order money-back guarantee applies to your first subscription shipment.

10. Acknowledgment email

Immediately after you enroll, we send a confirmation email that repeats the subscription terms, the amount and timing of the first and subsequent charges, the minimum commitment (none), how to cancel, and a direct link to the cancellation page — in keeping with Cal. Bus. & Prof. Code § 17602(a)(3) and ROSCA § 8403(2).
