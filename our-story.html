Our story

Three friends. One pouch.

A long question about what gut-first could actually feel like, if you took the supplement aisle out of it.

The question

Why this, and why now.

The LA customer we built this for has tried everything. Kombucha, pills, powders that foam in a shaker bottle, the $13 Erewhon smoothie that tastes like a multivitamin. None of it stuck — not because she stopped caring about her gut, but because the category kept handing her a chore disguised as a ritual.

We wanted gut health that felt like a pleasure, not a project. Something you'd actually keep on the counter, reach for without thinking, drink in five seconds, and forget about until it was time to order more. A pouch that earns its place the way a good olive oil or a well-designed kettle does — by being better-looking, better-tasting, and quietly effective.

That's PHEEEW. A 100mL puree pouch with a real synbiotic base, in a format that fits how you already live. Gut + Energy first, because that's the moment the day bends. Gut + Digestion and Gut + Immunity next. One architecture, one base, a sequence you already know how to ask for.

The three of us

Operator, obsessive, designer.

Three roles that had to work, three people who actually did.

If the pouch you just opened tastes exactly like the last one, that's him. If the box arrived on your porch the day the app said it would, that's also him. Market weekends are his, too.

Korean-American design as a principle, not a marketing angle. Quiet rigor. She doesn't sign a decision she doesn't believe in.

LA, Seoul, and the distance in between

A brand built in two time zones.

PHEEEW lives in two cities at once. Alex is in LA — born in Korea, raised in Seattle, now here. Jason is in LA. Dawon is in Seoul. The pouch is made in Naju, Korea and shipped out of a 3PL in LA. When LA wakes up, Seoul is wrapping. When Seoul starts, LA is heading to bed. The handoff runs all day.

That's not just where we are. It's how the brand actually looks and sounds. The Korean heritage is in the rigor — the restraint, the attention to the pouch in your hand, the refusal to over-talk. The Seattle part of Alex's arc shows up as quiet, warm, PNW-understated. The LA part is where it lives: on a counter, in a tote, at a Saturday market, in your morning. Three cities, one pouch, one gut.

The qualities we hold ourselves to are small and plain. Humble, because we're three friends with no war chest. Local, because we're made where we live and sold where we walk. Hustle, because the booth comes before the bottle shop. Calm, because the category is loud enough already. Real, because real fruit, real names, real email — hello@pheeew.com is Alex, and he answers.

That's the whole design principle. Not a zip code, not a vibe tag. A posture — Korean-American, LA-lived, Seoul-designed, made by hand until it can't be anymore.

What we're not

Not a wellness shot. Not a supplement. Not another Poppi.

We lead with feeling, not mechanism. We don't say "clinical." We don't run BFCM. We don't promise to fix anything the body is supposed to fix on its own. A pouch that tastes like real fruit, on a real synbiotic base, that fits in a bag. That's the whole pitch.

What's next
