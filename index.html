Standard probiotics have a survival problem.

Most commercial probiotics use strains like Lactobacillus and Bifidobacterium. These are legitimate bacteria — but they're fragile. They degrade in heat, lose potency on shelves, and most critically, they're destroyed by stomach acid before reaching your gut.

Studies show that standard probiotics can lose 60-90% of their viable organisms before they ever reach your intestines. You're paying for bacteria that arrive dead.

OUR SOLUTION

Spore-forming probiotics: nature's survival mechanism.

Bacillus coagulans is a spore-forming bacterium. When conditions are hostile — extreme heat, stomach acid, shelf storage — it forms a tough endospore shell around itself. A biological suit of armor.

This spore is remarkably resilient. It survives manufacturing, shipping, shelf life, and your stomach acid. When it reaches the favorable environment of your gut, it germinates — breaking out of its shell and becoming a fully active, live probiotic.

This isn't marketing. It's biology. And it's backed by over 50 years of published science.

Sources: Spore-forming probiotic safety and efficacy: FDA GRN 597, FDA GRN 864. Strain history: Sanzyme Biologics.

FAQ

Everything you want to know about Pheeew, our ingredients, subscriptions, and why this is different.

What is Pheeew?
Pheeew is The Synbiotic Smoothie — a shelf-stable fruit puree pouch packed with a live spore-forming probiotic (Bacillus coagulans SNZ 1969), prebiotic fiber (XOS + Fibersol-2L), vitamins, and real fruit. It's classified as conventional food, not a supplement. Think of it as a smoothie that also happens to be incredible for your gut.
What makes Pheeew different from other probiotics?
Three things. First, we use a spore-forming probiotic that actually survives your stomach acid — most standard probiotics don't. Second, we're a real fruit smoothie, not a pill or capsule, so it tastes amazing. Third, we're a synbiotic — we include both prebiotics (food for good bacteria) and probiotics (the good bacteria itself), so they work together for maximum effect.
Why did you pivot from juice to smoothie/puree?
Honestly? Taste. The puree format lets us use real fruit in a way that makes Pheeew taste 10,000x better than the juice version. The thicker texture also allows better incorporation of our probiotic and prebiotic ingredients, giving you a more effective product that you'll actually look forward to drinking every day.
What is a spore-forming probiotic?
Spore-forming probiotics create a tough protective shell (called an endospore) around themselves when conditions are harsh. This means they survive manufacturing heat, shelf storage, and — critically — your stomach acid. Once they reach the favorable environment of your gut, they break out of the shell and become fully active live bacteria. It's like biological armor. Standard probiotics (Lactobacillus, Bifidobacterium) don't have this ability, which is why many die before reaching your gut.
What does Pheeew taste like?
Like a really good fruit smoothie. Seriously. Tropical Punch tastes like a tropical vacation (mango, pineapple, passion fruit). Ginger Pear is warm and spicy-sweet. Wild Berries is a rich berry blend. We spent months on formulation because we believe the best health product is one you actually enjoy. You won't taste the probiotics or prebiotics — just delicious fruit.
Is Pheeew a supplement?
No. Pheeew is classified as conventional food by the FDA, not a dietary supplement. This means it's manufactured to food safety standards (which are actually higher than supplement standards in many ways), and we can make structure/function claims about our ingredients. It also means the ingredients are GRAS (Generally Recognized As Safe).

Nope. One of the biggest advantages of spore-forming probiotics is that they're naturally shelf-stable. No refrigeration needed — before or after opening. Toss a pouch in your bag, your desk drawer, your gym bag. It's good for 18+ months at room temperature.
