FAQ

Questions? Answered.

What is a synbiotic?
A product with both prebiotics (food for good bacteria) and probiotics (live good bacteria). Most products have one or the other. Pheeew has both.
What makes your probiotic different?
We use Bacillus coagulans SNZ 1969, a spore-forming strain. It creates a protective shell that survives heat, shelf storage, and stomach acid. Standard probiotics often don't survive the trip to your gut. Ours does. 4+ published clinical trials. 50+ years of safety data.
Do I need to refrigerate it?
No. Shelf-stable. Keep it in your bag, desk, car, gym bag. That's the whole point.
How do I take it?
Squeeze the pouch. Drink it. Done. 100mL, about 5 seconds. No mixing, no blender, no water needed.
What does it taste like?
Real fruit. Not "health food" taste. Tropical Punch (mango, tropical fruit, banana), Ginger Pear (ginger, apple, pear), Wild Berries (blueberry, elderberry, cranberry). We spent months on taste — it actually tastes good.
Is it a supplement?
No. Pheeew is a conventional food, not a dietary supplement. We use a Nutrition Facts panel, not Supplement Facts. We're registered as a food product.
How often should I drink it?
One pouch per day. Daily consistency is how gut health works. Pick a time — morning, afternoon, whenever — and make it your routine.
Can I cancel my subscription?
Anytime. No commitments. No fees. No guilt trips. Skip, pause, or cancel from your account dashboard. We'd rather earn your next order than trap you.
Where is it made?
Manufactured in Korea by a certified facility (FDA-registered, HACCP-certified). Shipped to the US. Korean manufacturing = the same quality standard behind K-Beauty.
What's the subscription price?
$69/month for 30 pouches ($2.30/pouch). One-time purchase: $79 ($2.63/pouch). Subscribers save 15% and get free shipping.
What's in the prebiotic fiber?
XOS (xylo-oligosaccharides) + Fibersol-2L (resistant maltodextrin by Matsutani/ADM). Not inulin. Not chicory root fiber. We chose these specific fibers because they're clinically studied and gentle on the stomach.
Is it vegan / gluten-free?
Formulated without dairy and gluten. Final allergen testing and certifications in progress. Full allergen info will be on every label.
