Questions

Before you ask.

What is Pheeew?
A 100mL puree pouch that starts with the gut. Real fruit, synbiotic base (pre + pro + post), and a function you can feel on the day you drink it. We launch with Gut + Energy — gentle lift, no crash. Feeling first, science honestly in the back.
Why "Gut + (function)"?
Because gut is the constant and the function is what the day needs. It's how your group chat already talks — "something for energy," "something for digestion," "something for when you feel it coming on." We match that, instead of inventing a new vocabulary.
When does it launch?
Late August to September 2026, contingent on funding closing on time. We'd rather tell you the real window than promise a date we can't hold. Waitlist gets the first heads-up when the shipping date locks.
How much will it cost?
The hero tier is our 30-pack — $99 one-time, or $72 on subscription. That's about $2.40 a pouch, every morning, at a real sub discount (27% off). Smaller testers from $34, bigger household packs up to 120. Full ladder goes live at launch.
Where can I buy it first?
The farmers market, before the website. Starting at booths at the Hollywood Farmers Market, Santa Monica Farmers Market, Silver Lake Farmers Market, Melrose Trading Post and Atwater Village Farmers Market. DTC opens at pheeew.com after launch weekend — waitlist gets 48 hours before the public.
Is this a supplement?
No. It's a food-first drink with a synbiotic base. We don't sell it as a supplement, we don't sell it as medicine, and we don't use clinical language on the label. If you want the ingredient detail, it'll all be on the science page at launch — nothing hidden, nothing proprietary-blended.
How is this different from a wellness shot?
Wellness shots taste like homework. This tastes like fruit. Wellness shots ask you to push through; this is something you'd actually reach for. The synbiotic work happens whether you notice or not — we just make sure you don't flinch on the way in.
How do I get involved early?
Join the waitlist, follow @saypheeew, and come find us at the market. If you want to try an early pouch or host a tasting, reply to any of our emails — that inbox is actually read.
