Privacy Policy

Effective: April 20, 2026 · Last updated: April 20, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how PHEEEW, Inc. ("PHEEEW," "we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, shares, and protects personal information when you interact with pheeew.com, purchase our products, subscribe to our emails, or meet us at a farmers market.

The short version. We collect what we need to ship you a beverage, bill you correctly, and — if you opt in — send you email. We don't sell your information for money. We honor California's privacy laws and respect browser-level opt-out signals. Contact us any time at privacy@pheeew.com.

1. Who we are

PHEEEW, Inc. is a Delaware C-Corporation with its principal place of business in Los Angeles, California ([LA address — TBD before launch]). We make a 100mL food-first smoothie pouch sold direct-to-consumer on pheeew.com and in person at farmers markets. PHEEEW, Inc. is the "business" for purposes of the California Consumer Privacy Act and the data "controller" for purposes of comparable state laws.

This policy is published in compliance with the California Online Privacy Protection Act (CalOPPA) (Cal. Bus. & Prof. Code §§ 22575–22579) and the California Consumer Privacy Act as amended by the California Privacy Rights Act (CCPA/CPRA) (Cal. Civ. Code § 1798.100 et seq.).

2. Information we collect

Information you give us

  • Contact & order information: name, email, shipping and billing addresses, phone number (optional), order contents.
  • Account information: if you create an account, your login email and preferences (flavor, subscription cadence, skip/pause settings).
  • Payment information: your card is collected and processed by our payment providers (Stripe and/or Shopify Payments). We do not store full card numbers on our servers. We receive a tokenized reference and the last four digits for order look-up.
  • Support communications: emails you send to hello@pheeew.com or messages you send via our support channels.
  • Marketing preferences: email opt-in status, SMS opt-in status (if offered), and unsubscribe history.
  • User-generated content: reviews, photos, and testimonials you choose to share with us.

Information collected automatically

  • Device & usage data: IP address, browser type and version, operating system, device identifiers, referring URL, pages viewed, actions taken, timestamps.
  • Cookies and similar technologies: see our Cookie Policy for details on what we use and why.
  • Analytics: we use Google Analytics and Shopify's built-in analytics to understand how visitors use the site.
  • Advertising pixels: if we run paid advertising, we use the Meta (Facebook/Instagram) pixel and TikTok pixel to measure campaign effectiveness.

Information from third parties

  • Shopify provides order, cart, and customer data.
  • Recharge manages subscription billing data for subscription customers.
  • Klaviyo manages email lists and engagement data for subscribers.
  • Meta and Google may share conversion and audience data if you arrived via their ads.
  • Our 3PL [TBD — see Master Doc s44] handles warehousing and fulfillment; it receives the shipping address and order contents needed to ship your order.

3. How we use your information

  • Process and fulfill orders, including passing shipping information to our carriers and 3PL.
  • Manage subscription billing and scheduled shipments (for subscribers).
  • Provide customer support and respond to questions.
  • Send transactional messages (order confirmations, shipping notices, subscription reminders required under law).
  • Send marketing emails if you opt in. You can unsubscribe at any time via the link in any email.
  • Improve the site, our product line, and our customer experience using aggregated analytics.
  • Prevent fraud, secure our systems, and protect our users.
  • Comply with legal obligations, including tax, accounting, consumer-protection, and food-safety requirements.
  • Enforce our Terms of Service.

We do not use your personal information to make automated decisions that produce legal or similarly significant effects on you.

4. Who we share your information with

We share personal information only with the categories of recipients listed below, and only as necessary for the purposes described.

  • Payment processors: Stripe, Inc. and Shopify Payments (a service of Shopify Inc.) to process card transactions.
  • Ecommerce platform: Shopify Inc. hosts our storefront and checkout.
  • Subscription engine: ReCharge Payments, LLC manages subscription lifecycle.
  • Email & SMS marketing: Klaviyo, Inc.
  • Analytics: Google LLC (Google Analytics), Shopify.
  • Advertising partners: Meta Platforms, Inc. and TikTok, Inc. (if we run paid campaigns).
  • Fulfillment & shipping: our 3PL [TBD] and common carriers (USPS, UPS, FedEx).
  • Professional advisors: accountants, lawyers, and insurers, as needed.
  • Government and law enforcement: when we are legally required to disclose information (subpoena, court order, lawful request).
  • Corporate transactions: in connection with a financing, merger, acquisition, or asset sale, we may transfer personal information to the successor entity, subject to equivalent privacy commitments.

We do not sell your personal information for money. Some of our advertising integrations (such as the Meta pixel) involve the "sharing" of personal information for cross-context behavioral advertising as defined in the CPRA. You may opt out at any time — see California Privacy Rights.

5. How long we keep your information

  • Order records: for as long as your account is active, plus a minimum of three (3) years afterward for tax, accounting, warranty, and consumer-protection recordkeeping (see, e.g., Cal. Rev. & Tax. Code § 6066 et seq. and IRS recordkeeping norms).
  • Marketing lists: until you unsubscribe, and for a brief period after to honor suppression.
  • Support communications: typically up to three (3) years.
  • Aggregated / de-identified analytics: indefinitely.
  • Payment tokens: for as long as your payment method remains on file with our processor.

6. International transfers

PHEEEW currently ships only within the United States (50 states plus the District of Columbia). We do not market to, or knowingly accept orders from, residents of the European Economic Area, the United Kingdom, or other regions with cross-border transfer restrictions. Some of our vendors operate infrastructure outside the United States; where that occurs, those transfers are governed by the vendor's own data-protection commitments.

7. Children

Our site and products are intended for adults. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 16 and we do not target children in our marketing. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, email privacy@pheeew.com and we will delete it. (See the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act, 15 U.S.C. §§ 6501–6506.)

8. California residents — your rights

California residents have the following rights under the CCPA/CPRA (Cal. Civ. Code § 1798.100 et seq.):

  • Right to know what personal information we collect, the sources, purposes, and categories of third parties we share it with (Cal. Civ. Code § 1798.100, § 1798.110).
  • Right to delete personal information we have collected about you, subject to exceptions (Cal. Civ. Code § 1798.105).
  • Right to correct inaccurate personal information (Cal. Civ. Code § 1798.106).
  • Right to opt out of sale or sharing of personal information, including cross-context behavioral advertising (Cal. Civ. Code § 1798.120).
  • Right to limit use and disclosure of sensitive personal information (Cal. Civ. Code § 1798.121). We do not currently use sensitive PI for inferences about you outside the purposes permitted without a limit request.
  • Right to non-discrimination for exercising your rights (Cal. Civ. Code § 1798.125).
  • Shine the Light: California residents may request a list of third parties to whom we have disclosed personal information for those parties' direct-marketing purposes (Cal. Civ. Code § 1798.83).

To exercise these rights, visit our California Privacy Rights page or email privacy@pheeew.com. We will verify your request by matching information you provide against records we hold. We respond to verifiable requests within 45 days, extendable once by an additional 45 days with notice (Cal. Civ. Code § 1798.130(a)(2)).

9. Other US state rights

Residents of Virginia (VCDPA, Va. Code § 59.1-575 et seq.), Colorado (CPA, Colo. Rev. Stat. § 6-1-1301 et seq.), Connecticut (CTDPA, Conn. Pub. Act 22-15), Utah (UCPA, Utah Code § 13-61-101 et seq.), and other states with comprehensive privacy laws may have rights to access, delete, correct, and opt out of targeted advertising and profiling. To exercise these rights, email privacy@pheeew.com. We honor appeals under states that provide them.

10. GDPR / EU residents

We do not currently ship to, or intentionally market in, the European Economic Area or the United Kingdom. If you are an EEA or UK resident and believe we hold personal data about you, contact privacy@pheeew.com.

11. Security

We use commercially reasonable administrative, technical, and physical safeguards designed to protect personal information, including TLS encryption in transit, access controls, and reliance on reputable vendors (Shopify, Stripe) that maintain industry-standard security practices. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure; we cannot guarantee absolute security.

12. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Material changes will be announced at the top of this page and, for significant changes affecting previously collected personal information, by email to the address on file. The "Last updated" date reflects the most recent revision.
