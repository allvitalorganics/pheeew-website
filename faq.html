About the brand

What is PHEEEW?

PHEEEW is a 100mL puree pouch that starts with the gut. One synbiotic base, one format, a sequence of functional expressions. Phase 1 is Gut + Energy.

Read the whole story on About.

Who built it?

Three friends — Alex Kim (founder, CEO, LA), Jason Ko (co-founder, COO, LA), and Dawon Kim (co-founder, design lead, Seoul). No war chest, no creator audience. Built in Los Angeles, manufactured in Korea.

Where is it made?

Manufactured in Naju, Korea — HACCP-certified, FDA-registered. Shipped and warehoused in Los Angeles. The pouch is designed in Seoul and lives on a kitchen counter in LA.

Why the name?

It's an exhale. The name is literally the sound of relief — a clean lift, a deep breath, a moment that doesn't ask anything of you. Short enough to say at a booth, long enough to own.

What does "Your gut knows." mean?

Our working tagline — two meanings at once. Your gut, the organ, actually knows. And your gut, the instinct, knows when a brand is for you. Both readings get to be true.

About the product

What is Gut + Energy?

A 100mL pouch with a synbiotic base (pre + probiotic together) plus green tea extract (~50–75mg natural caffeine) and B-vitamins. Clean caffeine, real fruit, five seconds from pocket to done.

Full breakdown on the shop page.

How do I drink it?

Tear the top, squeeze into your mouth. That's the whole thing. Shelf-stable, so no fridge required, though it tastes great chilled.

How much caffeine is in it?

~50–75mg per pouch from green tea extract. For context: about half a small coffee. Enough for a real lift, not a jittery one.

Is it refrigerated?

No — PHEEEW is shelf-stable for 12 months. Tastes great cold, perfectly fine at room temp. Lives in a pantry, a bag, a car cup holder.

Can I take it every day?

Yes. The synbiotic base is designed for daily use, and most customers have one in the morning. If you want two, that's fine too — see the Committed 60-pack.

When will Digestion and Immunity launch?

Gut + Digestion targets ~Month 6 after Phase 1 launch. Gut + Immunity targets ~Month 9–12. Each one earns the next — we only launch what the first SKU tells us the customer actually wants. Roadmap →

About ingredients + science

What's a synbiotic?

A prebiotic and a probiotic together in one serving. The probiotic is the bacteria; the prebiotic is the fiber that feeds it. In most products you get one or the other. PHEEEW is both, in every pouch. Full explainer →

What's the probiotic strain?

Bacillus coagulans SNZ 1969 — a spore-forming probiotic that survives heat, shelf storage, and stomach acid. 1 billion CFU per pouch. FDA GRAS. Read why we picked it →

Are there allergens?

No dairy, no gluten, no artificial flavors or colors. Full ingredient list and Nutrition Facts panel ship on the pouch and lock on the shop page pre-launch.

Is it safe during pregnancy?

Gut + Energy contains ~50–75mg of caffeine from green tea extract per pouch. If you're pregnant or nursing, talk with your doctor before starting any caffeinated product — including this one.

Is it FDA approved?

Food isn't "FDA approved" — it's regulated. The Bacillus coagulans species is FDA GRAS (Generally Recognized As Safe). Our manufacturer is FDA-registered and HACCP-certified. We don't claim the finished product is FDA-approved because that would be wrong. Read what we do and don't claim →
