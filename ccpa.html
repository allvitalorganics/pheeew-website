California Privacy Rights

Effective: April 20, 2026 · Last updated: April 20, 2026

If you are a California resident, you have specific rights over the personal information PHEEEW, Inc. collects about you. This page explains those rights and gives you a way to exercise them. For the broader description of what we collect and why, see our Privacy Policy.

Two-sentence summary. We do not sell your information for money. If you want to stop "sharing" for cross-context behavioral advertising (for example, the Meta pixel), click Do Not Sell or Share below, and we will honor Global Privacy Control signals automatically.

Your rights under CCPA / CPRA

These rights derive from the California Consumer Privacy Act, as amended by the California Privacy Rights Act (Cal. Civ. Code § 1798.100 et seq.), with key definitions at Cal. Civ. Code § 1798.140.

Right to know

Request the categories and specific pieces of personal information we have collected, the sources, purposes, and categories of third parties (§ 1798.110).

Right to delete

Request deletion of personal information we have collected, subject to the statutory exceptions (§ 1798.105).

Right to correct

Request correction of inaccurate personal information we hold about you (§ 1798.106).

Right to opt out of sale or sharing

Direct us not to sell or share your personal information, including for cross-context behavioral advertising (§ 1798.120).

Right to limit use of sensitive PI

Direct us to limit the use and disclosure of sensitive personal information to specific business-necessary purposes (§ 1798.121).

Right to non-discrimination

We will not deny you service, charge a different price, or provide a different level of quality for exercising your rights (§ 1798.125).

Shine the Light

California residents may also request a list of the categories of personal information we have disclosed to third parties for those parties' direct-marketing purposes during the prior calendar year, under California Civil Code § 1798.83 ("Shine the Light"). Email privacy@pheeew.com with the subject "Shine the Light Request."

Do Not Sell or Share My Personal Information

PHEEEW does not sell your personal information for money. However, our use of advertising technology — such as the Meta pixel and similar tools — may constitute "sharing" under CPRA, because it can enable cross-context behavioral advertising.

You can opt out in any of the following ways:

  1. Submit a request using the form below.
  2. Send a Global Privacy Control (GPC) signal from your browser. We honor GPC automatically as a valid opt-out request, consistent with Cal. Civ. Code § 1798.135.
  3. Email us at privacy@pheeew.com with the subject "Do Not Sell or Share."

Submit a verifiable consumer request

Use the form below or email privacy@pheeew.com. We will acknowledge your request within 10 business days and respond within 45 calendar days. If more time is needed, we will notify you in writing and may extend by up to an additional 45 days (Cal. Civ. Code § 1798.130(a)(2)).

How we verify requests

To protect your information, we verify requests by matching the information you provide (such as your email and order history) against records we hold. For sensitive requests (deletion, access to specific pieces), we may ask for additional identifying information. We will not use information collected for verification for any other purpose.

Authorized agents

You can designate an authorized agent to submit a request on your behalf. The agent must provide written, signed authorization, and we may separately verify your identity. For deletion and specific-pieces requests, we may require you to directly confirm the request.

Appeals

If we deny your request, you may appeal by replying to our denial email or writing to privacy@pheeew.com with the subject "Appeal." We will respond within 45 days. If you are dissatisfied with the outcome, you may contact the California Attorney General at oag.ca.gov/privacy or the California Privacy Protection Agency at cppa.ca.gov.
