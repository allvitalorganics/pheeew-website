Accessibility Statement

Effective: April 20, 2026 · Last updated: April 20, 2026

We want pheeew.com to be usable by everyone, including people with disabilities. This statement explains our commitment, what we have done, what we know we still need to do, and how to tell us when we fall short.

Our commitment

PHEEEW, Inc. is committed to making pheeew.com accessible in line with the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.1 at Level AA, and to operating consistent with the expectations of Title III of the Americans with Disabilities Act (42 U.S.C. §§ 12181–12189) and the Unruh Civil Rights Act (Cal. Civ. Code § 51).

Features we have implemented

  • Semantic HTML structure (headings, lists, landmarks) so assistive technologies can parse the page.
  • Keyboard navigation across all primary interactive elements, with visible focus indicators.
  • Alt text on content imagery; decorative imagery marked so screen readers can skip it.
  • Color contrast targets meeting WCAG 2.1 AA (4.5:1 for body text, 3:1 for large text and UI chrome).
  • Responsive layout that reflows at 320 px minimum width and supports 200% zoom without horizontal scroll on body content.
  • ARIA labels on navigation and interactive controls where the visible text is insufficient.
  • Form fields with visible labels and validation messages that are announced to assistive technology.
  • Link text that communicates destination (no "click here").

Known limitations

We publish these honestly because you deserve to know what to expect:

  • We have not yet commissioned a third-party WCAG 2.1 AA audit. We plan to complete a baseline audit within the first 90 days of public launch.
  • Some embedded third-party content (such as Shopify's checkout, payment elements, and Klaviyo forms) may not fully match WCAG 2.1 AA. We will update these as the underlying vendors improve their accessibility.
  • Certain animated or decorative elements may flash or move; we honor the prefers-reduced-motion media query wherever feasible.
  • Some PDFs and external documents may not be fully tagged for assistive technology. If you need an accessible copy, email us and we will produce one.

Feedback and contact

If you encounter a barrier using our site or want to request information in an accessible format, please contact us. We will respond within five business days and work to remediate the issue.

Email: accessibility@pheeew.com (primary) or hello@pheeew.com
Mail: PHEEEW, Inc. · Attn: Accessibility · [LA address — TBD before launch]

Ongoing commitment

Accessibility is an ongoing effort. We test new features for accessibility before shipping, we re-audit the full site at least annually, and we treat credible accessibility complaints as priority bugs. If a particular page is a barrier for you, tell us and we will prioritize it.

A note on honesty. We don't claim to be fully conformant — that would be a claim we cannot yet substantiate. Our goal is WCAG 2.1 AA across the main storefront flow, and we will continue to work toward it.
