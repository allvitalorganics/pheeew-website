Shipping & returns

What to expect.

Free shipping on sub 30+

Core, Committed, and Household subscriptions ship free in the continental US. Starter sub ships at $3.99 flat. One-time orders ship at $5.99 flat — subscribe and it's on us.

Delivery timing

Orders out of our LA 3PL ship within two business days. Ground delivery lands in 3–7 days depending on zip. Subscription boxes arrive on or before your renewal date.

Damaged or wrong order

Food and drink are non-returnable — but if a pouch arrives damaged or the wrong order lands at your door, we send a replacement. Email hello@pheeew.com with a photo. We make it right.

Before you buy

Four quick ones.

When will my subscription ship?

Your first box ships within two business days of ordering. After that, boxes renew on the same date each cycle — monthly for Core, every two months for Committed, quarterly for Household. You'll get an email three days before each renewal so you can skip, swap, or cancel.

Can I skip or cancel?

Any time, two clicks. Log into your account, skip the next box, swap to a different size, or cancel entirely. No lock-in, no call-to-cancel, no guilt flow.

What's your return policy?

Food and drink are non-returnable for safety reasons. But if your order arrives damaged or we sent the wrong thing, email hello@pheeew.com with a photo and we'll send a replacement.

How long does delivery take?

3–7 business days in the continental US via ground shipping. West Coast usually lands in 3–4 days, East Coast closer to 6–7. We don't currently ship to AK, HI, or internationally — we're working on it.
