Quick answers

The six questions everyone asks.

When does my order ship?

Subscription orders ship in 2–4 business days from our LA warehouse. One-time orders ship the next business day. Tracking email goes out the moment a label is printed.

Full details on our shipping page.

How do I cancel my subscription?

Log into your account and hit "Cancel subscription." Two clicks. No phone call, no retention pop-up, no "are you sure." We mean it.

See subscription terms for the fine print.

What's in it? Any common allergens?

Synbiotic base (Bacillus coagulans SNZ 1969 · XOS · Fibersol-2) + real fruit purée + green tea extract + B6/B12. No dairy, no gluten, no artificial flavors or colors. The full ingredient list and Nutrition Facts panel ship on the pouch and lock on this page pre-launch.

Can I return it if I don't like it?

Yes. If your first order isn't for you, email hello@pheeew.com within 30 days and we'll refund it. You don't have to mail anything back.

Full policy on the refund page.

Is it safe during pregnancy?

PHEEEW contains ~50–75mg of caffeine from green tea extract per pouch. If you're pregnant or nursing, we recommend talking with your doctor before starting any caffeinated product — including this one.

Does it work with a sensitive stomach?

The prebiotic dose is intentionally low to minimize bloating, and spore-forming probiotics are generally well-tolerated. That said, if you have a clinical GI condition, we'd recommend starting with a Tester 10-pack rather than a subscription — and checking with your doctor if you're unsure.
