Launch letter

Why we're launching Gut + Energy first.

By Alex Kim · Apr 2026 · 4 min read

We had a choice. Launch with a full line — energy, digestion, immunity, sleep, maybe a kids pouch — and look like a real company on day one. Or launch with one pouch and actually have something to say about it.

We picked one.

Gut + Energy is the one you reach for first. It's the morning pouch, the thing you grab on the way out the door when coffee is too much and water is not enough. It's the pouch I wanted to exist when I was standing in front of a cooler in LA last year, holding a bottle of something green I didn't believe in, thinking about the ten dollars I was about to spend on a feeling I'd forget by lunch.

Building one SKU properly is hard. Building five is a way to hide. You can't hide behind a range when every unit is the same format, the same 100 mL pouch, the same synbiotic base. You have to make the function land. You have to earn the second one.

So here's our promise for Phase 1: we ship Gut + Energy. We get it into your hand and your bag and your morning. We listen. If it's right, people will ask us for the next one, and we'll know what it should be. If it's not right, we'll know that too, which is arguably more important.

Building one SKU properly is hard. Building five is a way to hide.

The category we're stepping into is loud. Shots that scream. Powders that promise the world. Pills you forget to take. Our plan is to be quieter than all of it. A pouch that tastes like real fruit, a clean caffeine lift that doesn't feel like a shot, a synbiotic base that does its work without asking you to think about it.

Gut + Digestion and Gut + Immunity are real. They're designed. They're waiting. You can read about them if you want — there are pages on this site already. But they come after. Not because we're being precious. Because that's how you build something that lasts.

If you're reading this before launch, thanks for being early. If you're reading it after, thanks for sticking around. Either way: we're three people, we have one pouch, we're starting in LA, and we're going to do this the long way on purpose.

Your gut knows.

— Alex
