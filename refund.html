Refund & Returns

Effective: April 20, 2026 · Last updated: April 20, 2026

If your first PHEEEW order isn't right for you, we'll refund it. If anything arrives damaged, we'll replace it. Here's the detail.

The short version. First-time customers have 30 days to ask for a full refund on their first order, keep or toss the pouches. For damaged or incorrectly-shipped orders, email us within 7 days of delivery and we'll send a replacement at no cost. For subscription cancellation, see Subscription Terms.

First-order money-back guarantee

If this is your first PHEEEW order (one-time or your first subscription shipment) and it isn't for you, email hello@pheeew.com within 30 days of delivery and we'll refund the product total. You do not need to return unused pouches. We limit this guarantee to one use per household.

Repeat orders

Because our pouches are a perishable food product, we do not accept returns of opened, consumed, or partially consumed product on repeat orders. If there's an issue with the product itself (quality, taste deviation, appearance), email us with a photo — we review every complaint and will refund or replace where warranted.

Damaged, defective, or wrong items

If your shipment arrives damaged, defective, or contains the wrong items:

  1. Email hello@pheeew.com within 7 days of delivery.
  2. Include your order number, a photo of the packaging and product, and a short description.
  3. We will send a replacement at no charge, or issue a refund if you prefer.

How refunds are issued

Approved refunds are issued to the original payment method within 5–10 business days after approval. Depending on your bank, it may take an additional 3–10 business days to appear on your statement. Shipping fees are non-refundable except for defective, damaged, or incorrectly-shipped orders.

Subscription refunds and cancellation

You can cancel, skip, or pause your subscription at any time from your account dashboard or by emailing hello@pheeew.com. See Subscription Terms for full detail, including cancellation steps that satisfy the California Automatic Renewal Law (Cal. Bus. & Prof. Code §§ 17600–17606) and the federal Restore Online Shoppers' Confidence Act (15 U.S.C. §§ 8401–8405). Refunds on subscription orders follow the rules above.

California residents

This policy is posted in compliance with California Civil Code § 1723, which requires retailers to conspicuously post their return policy. In addition, if you are a California consumer, nothing in this policy limits any right you have under the Song-Beverly Consumer Warranty Act (Cal. Civ. Code § 1790 et seq.) or the California Consumers Legal Remedies Act (Cal. Civ. Code § 1750 et seq.).

Exchanges

We do not currently offer direct exchanges. If you want a different cadence or pack size, cancel or change your subscription in your account dashboard and place a new order.
