Shipping Policy

Effective: April 20, 2026 · Last updated: April 20, 2026

Domestic US shipping only at launch. Orders typically process in 1–3 business days and deliver in 3–7 business days after that. Here's the detail.

The short version. We ship to all 50 US states plus DC. Standard shipping is free on subscription orders of 30 pouches or more; $5.99 flat on one-time orders. International shipping is not available at launch.

Where we ship

PHEEEW currently ships to the 50 United States and the District of Columbia. We support PO Boxes, APO, FPO, and DPO addresses via USPS. We do not currently ship to US territories (Puerto Rico, Guam, US Virgin Islands) or internationally.

Order processing

Orders placed Monday–Friday (excluding US federal holidays) typically ship within 1–3 business days of order confirmation from our third-party logistics partner in Southern California [3PL — TBD; see Master Doc s44]. You will receive a tracking email when your package leaves the warehouse.

Tracking

Every order receives a tracking number once it ships. If you haven't received a tracking email within 4 business days of ordering, check your spam folder and then contact hello@pheeew.com.

Address accuracy

You are responsible for providing a complete and accurate shipping address. We cannot reroute packages already in transit. If a package is returned to us as undeliverable due to an address error, we will contact you to arrange a re-ship at your expense or a refund minus original shipping.

Lost, damaged, or stolen packages

If tracking shows your package as delivered but you cannot locate it, please (1) check with neighbors and your building, (2) wait 48 hours (carriers occasionally mark items delivered early), and (3) contact hello@pheeew.com if it still hasn't appeared. We will work with you on a case-by-case basis. For damaged shipments, see our Refund & Returns Policy.

Theft of a delivered package from your property is not something we can recover. We recommend shipping to an address where someone can receive packages during the day.

Storage and shelf life

Our pouches are shelf-stable at room temperature. Store in a cool, dry place away from direct sunlight. Best-by date is printed on each pouch. Refrigerate after opening and consume promptly.

International shipping

International shipping is not supported at launch. If that changes, this page will be updated.
